Sound of Dissent

Mapping Protest Music and Political Movements Across the World

Project Overview

Sound of Dissent explores the powerful connection between protest music and political movements across different eras and cultures. This project presents an interactive timeline and world map highlighting key protest songs and the historical events they were tied to – from Africa to the USA, Russia, and beyond.
